About Us

North East Education Consultancy (NEEC), a prominent educational consultancy in the North-Eastern Region of India, came to formation in the year 2011.Since then, it took upon the role of bringing education within the reach of every deserving individual. With its main office located in Guwahati, We have gained a lot of recognition because of its continuous dedication towards guiding the students.

What we believe in

We believe in transformation. Transforming and redefining ourselves to provide better guidance to the students is our priority.

What we do

We listen, understand, and empathize with what a student is going through. We encourage, support and provide guidance to a deserving candidate in pursuing their career.

What we aim to achieve

We aim to provide our helping hand to every single deserving individual all over India and help them realize their dreams with less difficulty.

Why Choose NEEC?

We can be a great assistant in building up your career. You can get thorough and complete details of the courses that you want to pursue in the future. This educational consultancy can be a good career guide for you. It also assists those students who have trouble in getting admission in an institute even after securing the required rank. Furthermore, you can get full details of your chosen college prior to admission from a personal counsellor.

Admission Process

We can help and guide you during the admission in your desired institute. It can answer any queries that you have regarding your desired institute.

Career Counselling

We aim at knowing and understanding the requirements of a candidate and at helping him/her to get closer to their dreams.

Application Process

We assist a student during the application process as well. It fills up the admission forms on behalf of the students.

College Information

If you want to know more about your desired institute, We can provide all the information to you. This can help you to make better and sound decisions regarding your choice.

Career Development

We can be an ultimate choice for your career build-up and development. With proper guidance and support, it can help you boost up your career.

Entrance Examination Guidance

We also guides a candidate in cracking the entrance examinations and therefore to get admission in their desired college or institute.
